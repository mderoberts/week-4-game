Crystals Collector! A game made with jQuery

Instructions

  • When the game starts, you will receive a random number.
  • The four crystals below the scoreboard have different values, which are hidden until you click on one. Clicking on crystals adds points to your score.
  • You win by matching your score to the random number. You lose if your score goes above the random number.
  • Crystal values will change each time the game starts.
